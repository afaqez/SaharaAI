**About The Department**
------------------------

The department promotes excellence in pharmaceutical sciences by preparing students to meet the challenges of healthcare and pharmaceutical profession. Emphases is laid on developing a solid foundation of the discipline.

**Career Prospects**
--------------------

*   Pharmaceutical Industry
*   Drug Regulatory Authorities
*   Academia
*   Community/Retail Pharmacy
*   Clinical Pharmacy/Hospital Pharmacy
*   Nuclear Pharmacy
